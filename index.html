Movie Review

willy wonka, Dec 7, 2017

Willy wonka was a new movie about a chocolate maker named willy wonka. He faced lots of adversity in the movie while trying to make his chocolate. The bad guys didn't want him to make his chocolate cause it was so good. In return he fought back and made better chocolate. It was a pretty good movie.

Book Review

Maus, Sep 2, 2017

the book maus is a comic book about the holocaust. It was written by the son of a holocaust surviver and is pretty accurate. In the book the jewish people are depicted as mice and the nazis are cats. The book is written in two parts and has lots of flashbacks and different perspectives. It is overall a pretty good book
